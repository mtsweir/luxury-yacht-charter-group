Our Point of Difference

Our unique knowledge and experience of marine travel equips us to advise you with confidence about your dream charter. We'll match the right boat in the destination of your dreams.

Luxury Charter Group stands as an independent global charter organisation which has a vast and varied portfolio of yachts available for your consideration. The yachts we offer have already met our strict criteria by our own personal inspection. We inspect at least 400 yachts a year attending all the major international charter yacht shows worldwide, continually refreshing our knowledge to ensure you get the most up-to-date charter advice and expertise.

In 2015 our attendance at international charter shows includes inspection of yachts at Genoa, Greece, Turkey, Croatia, Cannes, Monaco, Tortola, Ft Lauderdale and Antigua.

Through our dedication and love of yachting, we have also personally explored and sailed many favourite charter destinations around the world. Most recently we have personally cruised British Virgin islands and Antigua in the Caribbean, Bahamas; Sicily and the Aeolian Islands, Amalfi Coast/ Gulf of Naples, Turkish Coastline, Greek Islands, Croatia's Dalmatian Coast in Europe and Galapagos, Tahiti, New Zealand and the Whitsunday islands of Australia in the South Pacific region.

From all of our experiences with charter totalling over 40 years, we pride ourselves in being able to offer you the best unbiased solution for your dream holiday on the water, and naturally with it comes our highly personalised service.
