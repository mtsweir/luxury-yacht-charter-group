Meet the Team

Jeni Tidmarsh

Email: jeni@...

Jeni Tidmarsh, the founder of The Luxury Charter Group, has built her company's enviable reputation on more than 35 years of sailing the waters of the Mediterranean, the Caribbean and the South Pacific, and through a strong alignment to the luxury charter yacht industry worldwide.

Jeni has had a wide range of yachting experience. Her highly successful business has emerged from her vast experience both  on and off the water. She has crewed on charter yachts; delivered yachts across the world's oceans; been a sailmaker; worked on the development of marinas; been the advertising manager for a yachting magazine; owned her own yacht which she cruised, raced extensively, and competed in the intense cauldron of one-on-one match racing.

Jeni brings this vast knowledge – a vibrant blend of marine and travel experiences – to her company.

Her remarkable success story with The Luxury Charter Group has evolved through a perfect amalgam – dedication to her clients, a genuine appreciation of their expectations for a luxury yacht charter experience, a highly valuable knowledge of the world's best cruising destinations, and through having a personally selected fleet of luxury yachts available for charter.

'Our passion is yachting and our pledge to our clients is to deliver an exclusive charter experience through professional and personalised service,' Jeni declares with pride. 'It is an approach that sees the Luxury Charter Group having an ever-increasing number of clientele returning to us for new adventures, and others who come to us through recommendations from our loyal clientele.

'We are always working in the interests of our charterers; constantly travelling the world, visiting existing charter destinations, looking for exciting new cruising opportunities, and inspecting all types of yachts for inclusion in our portfolio of luxury charter vessels.

Sharon Bahmer

Email: sharon@...

Sharon Bahmer, the Luxury Charter Group's Senior Charter Executive, is based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. The majority of her clients are resident in the USA; however she also has an extensive international clientele who desire charter destinations primarily in the Caribbean, Bahamas, USA and Mediterranean.

With three decades of experience across the complete spectrum of luxury yacht charters to her credit, Sharon's talents sit comfortably with the Luxury Charter Group's ethos – to create the perfect match between client, charter vessel and destinations.

Her life has always been associated with the sea. Sharon has cruised considerable distances under sail, even crossing the Atlantic from her native South Africa to Brazil on a small yacht, and subsequently voyaging to the most beautiful islands and destinations in the Caribbean. This lifestyle is so deeply entrenched that quite often she spends her vacations aboard sail and motor yachts, visiting far-flung, exciting and romantic destinations.

Sharon's professional standing within the luxury yacht charter industry in America sees her as a member of the Board of Directors and recently voted to Vice President of the Charter Yacht Brokers Association. She was previously Chair of that group's Education Sub-committee, a unit which has as its goal the education of charter brokers about issues and trends across the industry. Sharon is also a member in good standing of the Florida Yacht Brokers Association.

Radek Gendek

Email: radek@...

Based in Europe, Radek Gendek has joined Luxury Charter Group to manage the company's presence on the Old Continent and serve best our European clients.

Radek was born in Gdansk, Poland, the home of Sunreef Yachts shipyard, where he worked as charter director for several years. Fluent in English, French and Polish, Radek is a highly qualified charter professional whose insight and expertise ensures clients are matched with their ideal yacht and itinerary.

Along with his valuable first-hand experience of the world's most desirable charter destinations, Radek is a regular visitor to the boat shows of Europe, Carribean and the US, giving him insight into the latest cruising destinations, charter yacht fleets and crew.
