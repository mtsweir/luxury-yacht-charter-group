Our Customers

Our customer Feedback

We at Luxury Charter Group highly value our customer's opinions on the services we provide, the yachts we feature and the destinations we showcase. Our reviews and feedback section helps us to continually improve and evolve as a company to better tailor our services to the likes and needs of our client base.

Over the years we have taken enormous pride in receiving some positive and encouraging feedback from our customers. So, if you need to be reassured about the quality of our services, please take a look below and see what some of our clients are saying about the services & experiences we offer… you might be joining them next!

ALEGRIA

British Virgin Islands

SHARON Our charter on ALEGRIA was fantastic! Nathan and Penny were the absolute best crew in so many ways. Sherrie and I have chartered over 15 times in the BVI's and this one rates as one of the best. The boat was new, clean and primo in every way. I highly recommend this boat to others but even more the crew. Thank you Sharon for putting this all together for us - I have already given your name and Luxury Charter Group to several interested friends. Hope to charter with you again very soon.

We thoroughly enjoyed our time & are very grateful

Thank you for all your help in making our trip run so smoothly.
